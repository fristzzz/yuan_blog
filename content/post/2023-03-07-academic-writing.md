# Style

1. Avoid verb + prep (verb instead)
2. Avoid idiomatic惯用的 language
3. Avoid contractions
4. Avoid run-on expression (etc, so on)(use 'such as')
5. Avoid addressing the reader as "you"
6. Avoid sexist words

don't intensify
